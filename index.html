Important: SSVWP (short stay visa waiver program) Note)
Because Jenifer enters the UK first on a UK Standard Visitor Visa, she can travel to the Republic of Ireland without a separate Irish visa — this is the Short Stay Visa Waiver Programme. The key is that the UK must be the first point of entry. This itinerary is designed exactly for that: Manila → London (enter UK) → Edinburgh → Belfast → Republic of Ireland. The border crossing at Newry is completely open under the Common Travel Area — no checks, no passport control. For the visa application, submit this full itinerary with hotel names, travel connections, and Jenifer's IBM employment letter showing ties to return to the Philippines.
